Butter Chicken Recipe

  • ingredients
  • method
  • nutrition

Ingredients

  • chicken breast/thigh
  • yoghurt
  • butter
  • cumin
  • onions
  • tomatoes

Method

  1. cut the chicken into small chunks
  2. in a bowl, create the marinade for the chicken using all the spices and the yoghurt, then throw the chicken in and ensure it is all covered
  3. using butter in the frying pan, brown the chicken
  4. remove the chicken from the pan and add more butter to fry your onions until golden
  5. add your tomatoes and fry until they mush
